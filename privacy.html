Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

ClaudeKarma does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Your usage information stays on your device.

Overview

ClaudeKarma is a browser extension that displays your Claude AI usage statistics in the browser toolbar. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

ClaudeKarma does not collect any data. We do not gather personal information, browsing history, analytics, tracking data, cookies, or transmit anything to external servers.

How It Works

ClaudeKarma reads your Claude AI usage data directly from claude.ai using your existing browser session. This data:

  • Is fetched directly from claude.ai to your browser
  • Is stored locally on your device only
  • Is never sent to any external servers
  • Is never shared with third parties

Local Storage

The extension stores preferences and cached data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This includes:

  • Usage data (session and weekly usage percentages)
  • Organization ID (for API requests to claude.ai)
  • User preferences (tips page viewed status)

This data never leaves your device.

Permissions

ClaudeKarma requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Host permission (claude.ai) — to fetch your usage data from Claude's API
  • Storage — to save usage data and preferences locally
  • Alarms — to periodically refresh usage data in the background

Third-Party Services

ClaudeKarma uses no third-party services, analytics, or tracking. The only external communication is between your browser and claude.ai (Anthropic's official website) to fetch your usage data.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
