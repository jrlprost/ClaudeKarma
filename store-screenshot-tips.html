Built-in Tips & Tricks

Master the art of prompting with curated best practices directly inside the extension.

Be Specific Provide clear context and constraints. The more specific your prompt, the better Claude's response.
Use Examples Show Claude what you want with input/output examples. Few-shot prompting dramatically improves results.
Iterate & Refine Build on previous responses. Ask Claude to adjust, expand, or change specific parts of its answer.
Break It Down Split complex tasks into smaller steps. Claude handles focused requests more effectively.
Assign a Role Tell Claude to act as an expert. "You are a senior developer" produces more specialized answers.
Set the Format Request specific output formats: bullet points, tables, code blocks, or step-by-step guides.
